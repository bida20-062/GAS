About Us

GAS 'N' PAY is a gas distribution company owned by a partnership between Mr Fred Banyatsi, Ms Ludo Kenosi, Mr Sean Mmolai and Ms Katlego Ntalabgwe. Since its inception in 2018, the company has been in the hands of its first generation of proprietors. We are proud of the commitment we show our clients by genuinely understanding their gas delivery and servicing needs. Our major business is to provide peace of mind to households who are having difficulty obtaining gas delivery services, and we do it at a lower cost. As a result, the majority of Gaborone people have put their faith in us to bring high-quality gas to their homes. Henceforth, our firm was subsequently acknowledged as one of the greatest gas supply companies in the city.
